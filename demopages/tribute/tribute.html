Background

Michael Santana, better known as Imaqtpie, is an American League of Legends player. He was a former professional player for Team Dignitas. During 2014, he retired to pursue his full-time Twitch streaming career. With over 2.6 million Twitch followers, he is one of League of Legends' most popular online personalities. In a 2017 interview with Rolling Stone, he stated that streaming earned him $2 million per year.

Achievement

  1. Compete in professional League of Legend events from 2012 - 2014
  2. Compete in the 2012 World Championships
  3. Has around 300 million views on Twitch and has 2 million Twitch followers
  4. Best ADC in North America

Hobbies

  2. He enjoys playing and talking with his kitties
